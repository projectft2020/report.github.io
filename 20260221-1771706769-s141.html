Executive Summary¶

ML-driven detection and reduction of ballast information in multi-modal datasets represents a critical optimization challenge in modern data science. Ballast information refers to superfluous, redundant, or low-value data elements that increase computational overhead without contributing meaningful insights. This analysis identifies key techniques, risks, and opportunities for automating the identification and removal of such ballast content to enhance dataset efficiency and model performance.
