# Template 1: Stock Screening Framework

**Category:** Stock Screening
**Role:** Equity Research Analyst (20+ years experience)
**Language:** English
**Usage:** Select 10 most suitable stocks based on investment criteria

---

## Prompt Template

```
You are an experienced equity research analyst with over two decades of experience advising institutional and high-net-worth investors on stock selection. Create a comprehensive stock screening framework tailored to my investment objectives.

Your analysis should include:
• A list of 10 most suitable stocks based on my criteria, including ticker symbols
• P/E ratio evaluation and how each company compares with its sector average
• Revenue growth performance across past five years
• Balance sheet health using debt-to-equity ratio
• Dividend yield analysis and an assessment of payout sustainability
• Competitive advantage assessment (rate as: weak, moderate, or strong moat)
• 12-month upside and downside scenarios including bull and bear price targets
• Risk score for each company on a scale from 1–10 with a brief explanation
• Suggested entry price ranges and potential stop-loss levels

Format as a professional equity research screening report with summary table.

My investment profile:
[DESCRIBE YOUR RISK TOLERANCE, INVESTMENT AMOUNT, TIME HORIZON, AND PREFERRED SECTORS]
```

---

## Key Output Fields

- **Stock List:** 10 stocks with ticker symbols
- **P/E Analysis:** Compare to sector average
- **Revenue Growth:** 5-year performance
- **Balance Sheet:** Debt-to-equity ratio
- **Dividend:** Yield and sustainability
- **Moat Rating:** Weak / Moderate / Strong
- **Price Targets:** 12-month bull/bear scenarios
- **Risk Score:** 1-10 scale with explanation
- **Entry/Stop Loss:** Suggested levels

---

## Example Usage

```
You are an experienced equity research analyst with over two decades of experience advising institutional and high-net-worth investors on stock selection. Create a comprehensive stock screening framework tailored to my investment objectives.

My investment profile:
Risk Tolerance: Moderate
Investment Amount: $50,000
Time Horizon: 3-5 years
Preferred Sectors: Technology, Healthcare, Consumer Discretionary
```

---

## Tips for Best Results

1. **Be Specific About Risk Tolerance:** 
   - Conservative: Focus on large-cap, stable companies
   - Moderate: Mix of growth and value
   - Aggressive: Small-cap, high-growth stocks

2. **Include Sector Preferences:**
   - Helps narrow down relevant industries
   - Reduces irrelevant recommendations

3. **Specify Time Horizon:**
   - Short-term (<1 year): Focus on momentum/catalysts
   - Medium-term (1-3 years): Balance growth and value
   - Long-term (>5 years): Focus on competitive moats

4. **Mention Investment Amount:**
   - Helps with position sizing recommendations
   - Affects diversification suggestions
