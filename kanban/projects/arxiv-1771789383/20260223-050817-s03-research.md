## Detailed Analysis

### Company Overview and Business Model
Warner Bros. Discovery is a leading global media and entertainment company creating and distributing branded content across television, film, streaming and gaming. The company operates in more than 220 countries and territories across 50 languages, with iconic brands including:
- Discovery Channel, Max, discovery+
- CNN, HBO, DC
- TNT Sports, Eurosport
- HGTV, Food Network, TLC
- Warner Bros. Motion Picture Group, Warner Bros. Games
- Cartoon Network, Adult Swim, and many others

WBD's business model spans traditional linear television, streaming services (Max), theatrical distribution, home entertainment, and gaming.
