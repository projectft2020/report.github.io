### Moving Average Filters for Futures Signals

**Standard Parameters and Applications:**

1. **Closing Price Filter:**
   - Require 1-3 successive closes above/below the moving average
   - Eliminates false signals during ranging markets
   - Most effective for ES and NQ on 5-15 minute timeframes

2. **Slope Direction Filter:**
   - Moving average must slope in the direction of the trade
   - Works with simple moving averages but not exponential
   - Provides trend confirmation for swing trading strategies

3. **Multi-Bar Clearance Filter:**
   - Require 2-3 complete bars clear of the moving average
   - Reduces whipsaws in volatile conditions
   - Particularly effective for GC and NQ futures

4. **Time Frame Confluence:**
   - Higher timeframe moving averages provide strong support/resistance
   - Daily gaps show stronger magnetic effects in ES and NQ
   - Multi-timeframe analysis confirms institutional participation

**Signal Quality Improvements:**
- Combine multiple filters to reduce false signals by 60-70%
- Use exponential moving averages for recent price emphasis
- Apply ATR-based moving averages for volatility adaptation
- Incorporate volume confirmation for breakout scenarios
